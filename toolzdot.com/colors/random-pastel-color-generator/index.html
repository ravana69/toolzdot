What are pastel colors and why are they popular?

Pastel colors are a type of color that is typically soft, light, and muted. These colors are usually created by adding white to a hue, which makes them appear paler. Pastel colors are popular for many different reasons. They can add a touch of elegance to any setting, and they also have a calming effect. Pastel colors can be used in both interior design and fashion.

What determines the hue of a pastel color?

The hue of pastel color is determined by the amount of white that is added to the original hue. The more white that is added, the lighter and paler the color will become. Some common pastel hues include pink, blue, and yellow.
